# Atlas City Dashboard v1.0

The Dashboard is the central cockpit for every citizen to manage their technical identity and economic assets.

## CORE MODULES

### 1. My Building Preview
- **Logic**: Dynamic SVG generation based on DIS score.
- **Interactions**:
  - Pulse animation on score update.
  - Progress bar towards next architectural milestone (+1 floor).
  - Quick-link to spatial position in `/city`.

### 2. City Credits (Economic Layer)
- **Claim Daily**: Retention mechanism for consistent citizenship.
- **Balance History**: Real-time log of earning/spending events.

### 3. Badge Showcase (Social Signaling)
- **Unlock Mechanics**: Automated verification of technical milestones.
- **Rarity**: Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary phenotypes.

## USER TIPS
- **Level Up**: Connect external accounts (Github, YouTube) to accelerate building growth.
- **Social Influence**: Upvote other citizens in the Voting Panel to earn social reciprocity.
- **City Map**: Use the "Ver na Cidade" button to see your status in the global landscape.
