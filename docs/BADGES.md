# Atlas City Achievements (Badges v1.0)

Badges are visual indicators of your technical expertise, community contribution, and social influence in Atlas City.

## INITIAL BADGES (FASE 0)

| Badge | Requirement | Rarity | Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primeiro Território** | Reclamar um lote no mapa | Common | 50 CC |
| **Dev Ativo** | 10 commits em uma semana | Rare | 200 CC |
| **Pioneiro** | Estar entre os primeiros 100 usuários | Legendary | 500 CC |
| **Conquistador** | Score DIS ≥ 5000 | Epic | 300 CC |
| **Conector** | 3+ plataformas conectadas | Common | 100 CC |
| **Empresário** | Perfil de Startup verificado | Rare | 250 CC |
| **Influencer** | Trust Score ≥ 0.8 | Epic | 400 CC |
| **Open Source** | 5+ repos públicos | Rare | 150 CC |
| **Consistente** | 7 dias seguidos de claim diário | Common | 75 CC |
| **Embassador** | 5+ convites aceitos | Legendary | 600 CC |

## HOW IT WORKS
1. **Detection**: The Atlas City engine automatically detects when you meet a requirement.
2. **Notification**: You will see a toast notification when a badge is unlocked.
3. **Claiming**: Go to your Dashboard → Badge Showcase and click "CLAIM" to receive your City Credits.

## VISUAL SIGNALING
Badges are displayed on your public profile (`/@username`), allowing other citizens to gauge your reliability and expertise at a glance.
