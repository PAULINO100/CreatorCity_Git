# Atlas City Economic Layer (v1.0)

City Credits (CC) are the primary off-chain utility token used to reward citizens for their active participation in the megacity.

## REWARD TABLE

| Action | Reward | Frequency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Daily Login** | 10 CC | Every 24 hours |
| **GitHub Commit** | 15 CC | Per verified commit |
| **YouTube Upload** | 25 CC | Per technical video |
| **New Platform Link** | 100 CC | Once per platform |
| **Invite Friend** | 50 CC | Per successful referral |
| **Achievement Badge** | 200 CC | per unique milestone |

## RULES & POLICIES
1. **Off-Chain**: All credits are currently off-chain and localized to the Atlas City database.
2. **Reputation Tie**: High reputation scores (DIS) may unlock limited-edition items or higher daily multipliers in the future.
3. **Daily Claim**: The timer starts from the moment of your last claim.
4. **Fraud Prevention**: Automated activity (e.g. commit spamming) is flagged by the Reputation Engine and may result in balance forfeiture.

## UPCOMING FEATURES
- **Marketplace**: Buy custom SVG assets for your building.
- **District Boosters**: Spend CC to temporarily boost your district's visual prominence on the 2D Map.
- **Credit Swaps**: Potential future bridge to on-chain ecosystems.
